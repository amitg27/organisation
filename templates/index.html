The Organization of Choice™ (OOC) is an independent global assessment and recognition program. We certify organizations — of every size, sector, and geography — that demonstrate genuine excellence across the dimensions that matter most to people at work: leadership, culture, wellbeing, growth, communication, and purpose.

OOC certification is not a popularity vote, a paid placement, or a self-declared award. It is an evidence-based recognition earned through a structured multi-stakeholder assessment — one that asks your employees, your management, your leadership, and your customers what they truly experience.
