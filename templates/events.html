What We Run

Our Events

OOC Annual Recognition Summit

Our flagship annual event. Certified organizations are celebrated in person, keynote speakers share research on the future of work, and HR leaders from around the world connect. Held annually in a rotating global city.

Certification Nation Day

A global day of celebration for all OOC-certified organizations. Share your certification milestone, join live sessions, and connect with a worldwide community of recognized workplaces.

Assessment Webinars

Free monthly webinars for organizations considering OOC certification. Our team walks through the assessment process, answers questions, and shares what the best-performing organizations have in common.

CHRO & People Leader Roundtables

Invitation-only conversations for certified organizations. Small-group discussions on the challenges and opportunities facing people leaders in today's workplace.
