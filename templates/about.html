About

We Believe Every Great Workplace
Deserves to Be Known

Organization of Choice was built for one reason: to give the world's best workplaces the independent recognition they have earned — regardless of their size, industry, or location.

Our Story

Why We Built This

Across the world, there are organizations doing extraordinary work for their people. Organizations that invest in their culture, lead with integrity, listen to their employees, and build something their teams are genuinely proud of.

Yet most of these organizations go unrecognized — either because existing recognition programs are too expensive, too complex, or built only for the largest enterprises.

Organization of Choice was built to change that. We created a program that is rigorous enough to mean something, accessible enough for organizations of every size, and global enough to matter wherever your organization operates.

OOC

"Rigorous enough to mean something. Accessible enough for every organization."

Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To recognize organizations worldwide that have built workplaces where people genuinely choose to belong — and to help all organizations aspire to that standard.

Our Vision

A world where every organization — from a ten-person startup to a ten-thousand-person enterprise — has access to a credible, meaningful standard of workplace excellence.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Independence

OOC is not affiliated with any employer, recruiting platform, or commercial interest. Our assessments are conducted independently and our recognition reflects only what organizations genuinely earn.

Accessibility

World-class recognition should not be limited to the world's largest organizations. OOC is designed for organizations of all sizes — and priced to reflect that.

Integrity

No paid placements. No sponsored rankings. No recognition without assessment. Every OOC-certified organization has earned their status through the same rigorous process.

Impact

Recognition without insight is just a trophy. OOC certification comes with a detailed assessment report that helps organizations understand exactly where they excel — and where to grow.
